The Four Divisions

One integrated ecosystem. Four specialist areas. Label-standard support across the full creative journey.

01

The Content Lab

Short-Form Production · Creative Studio

We create the content that stops the scroll. Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, visualizers, documentaries, and full creative packages for releases and brand campaigns.

  • Reels, TikTok & YouTube Shorts
  • Visualizers & storytelling clips
  • Filming, editing & creative packaging
  • Documentary & long-form content

The Audience Lab

Digital Marketing · Audience Growth · Strategy

We build audiences and drive streams using data, platform strategy, and creative campaigns built for how people actually discover music today.

  • Social media strategy & content calendars

The Creative Desk

We develop artists holistically — from brand identity and A&R to release planning, creative oversight, and long-term career architecture.

The Sound Desk

Music Curation · Playlist Programming · Sonic Strategy

We shape how music is sequenced, heard, and experienced — across streaming platforms, live events, and brand activations.
